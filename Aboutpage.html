About Us

Hi! We are a group of four women who decided to work together on this project for TechTogether Miami. Though we are not Miami locals, two of us are from Orlando, FL. We all were very interested in portraying the space scene in Miami and Florida in general, and wanted to focus on a site that would cater to all space enthusiasts.
