  • Constellations are groups are stars that appear as objects or creatures in the sky.
  • There are currently 88 "official" constellations.
  • Most constellations were influenced by ancient Middle Eastern, Greek, and Roman cultures.
  • An asterism is a recognized grouping of stars that isn't an official constellation, like The Big Dipper.
  • Yes, you read that right. The Big Dipper is not an officially recognized constellation.
